About Me

My name is Alana Vodicka and I'm a freshman in college at the University of South Carolina. I'm studying psychology with a minor in biology on the pre-physical therapy track. Here at school I am in the sailing club. I am the social media and public relations chair on the executive board of sailing, so I run all of our social media accounts and take pictures. During racing season I travel with the sailing team to other schools in the area to compete in regattas.

I am originally from Northern Illinois. I'm very close with my family which consists of my parents and my younger sister Alyssa. Alyssa and I used to run cross country together in high school before I graduated. My family also enjoys spending time together on the water. We live up the street from a beach where we hang out with family friends and launch our boat. On the boat my whole family loves to go water skiing, including my grandparents, aunts, and uncles.

My Future Career Plans

My career plan is to become a physical therapist. I'm interested in studying physical therapy because I have been very involved in sports my whole life and after experiencing an injury where I had to do physical therapy for about 7 months I became fascinated with it. I think understanding the way the human body works and can heal from injuries is incredibly interesting. I want to go into this career so that I can not only do something I enjoy, but also to be able to help others. I'd like to have a career where I can make a difference in other people's lives and physical therapy is a good option for that.

I'd like to go to graduate school somewhere on the east coast, but I'm not sure exactly where yet. After I graduate I'd like to move west and work somewhere in Colorado or Oregon. I think a really cool job to pursue would be working for a professional sports team as a part of their physical therapy team. In this I would be able to work specifically with athletes and build relationships with the team.
